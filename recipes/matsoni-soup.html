Matsoni Soup

Description

This satisfying, slightly tart yogurt-based soup with rice can be enjoyed hot or cold.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sunflower oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • White rice
  • Matsoni (yogurt)
  • Water
  • Fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper
  • Arajani (sour cream)

Steps

  1. Cook the rice.
  2. Saute the onions and garlic in butter and oil.
  3. Add yogurt, rice, and water and bring to a boil.
  4. Season with dill, salt, and peeper.
  5. Add water and sour cream as needed to reach desired consistency.
