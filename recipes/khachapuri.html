Khachapuri

Description

This scrumptious cheesy bread will keep you coming back for more.

Ingredients

Dough

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Water

Filling

  • Mozarella cheese, shredded
  • Feta cheese, crumbled

Steps

  1. Combine lukewarm water and yeast, allow to proof.
  2. Add to flour, mix, kneed, and let rise.
  3. Roll dough into a flat circle.
  4. Add cheese to center.
  5. Fold dough over from outside to center all around the circle.
  6. Roll out flat.
  7. Bake in the oven or on the stovetop in a cast iron pan.
